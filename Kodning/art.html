Covid art

In this project, I had to develop a work of art that would be used for a teaching course in primary school. I had to make a pice of art, inspirred by CovidArt from instagram. I had to make a hand out of silver paper and put it in a suitable environment. The hand illustrates a fist beating covid particles.

You can read more about the course here: https://emu.dk/grundskole/corona-gode-raad-til-undervisning/praktisk-musiske-fag/7-8-klasse-covid-art or you can read about it in this book: Visual arts, handbook for electives and samples 2021 by Rikke Hyldahl Homann and Marlene Muhlig p. 31

Memory box

This project was made as an illustation to a school project in primary school, here i had to make a memory box that would show how to use a micro:bit in a creative way. I had to take a good memory, make it more living by using pensels and other things, then take the picture and put it into a box. I then made a lid for the box, and had to program the micro:bit so the lid would open by entering the correct code, and close a few seconds later.

The progaming af the micro:bit was made in https://makecode.microbit.org

Papmache baby

In this project, I had going to make a baby out of papmache. The baby was to be used for an exam project, where the students had to have an objeckt to look at. I had to make the baby sit in the fetal position and out of its umbilical cord some of the world's problems would appear. This should symbolize the problems amperson is born into. I started by making the baby's shape out of steel wire, to give me a skillet that I could shape the rest of the baby around. Then I started putting the papmah around and shaping its feet, hands as well as face. Finally, I shaped and put its umbilical cord on.
